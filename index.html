Few words about us

The Platform Organization (herein referred to as TPO) is a Non-Profit making organization that advocates for and supports youth and women's participation in development processes in Kenya.

We currently implement projects in Siaya County and other counties within Kenya with a focus on youth, women, and Person Living with Disabilities (PLWDâ€™s). To achieve impact at scale, The Platform Organization influences strong youth and women participation in Agribusiness, Climate Change, governance processes and holds county governments accountable for quality service delivery as well as improving their livelihoods.

We also seek to strengthen youth participation in the development agenda in line with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) targets at County, National, Regional and Global levels
